A strong password is the first line of defense against unauthorized access to your online accounts. It's essential to create passwords that are unique, complex, and difficult to guess. A good password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using easily guessable information such as your name, birthdate, or common words. It's also a good idea to change your passwords regularly, especially for sensitive accounts like email, banking, and social media.
